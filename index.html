VoiceVault

Consent-based voice rights and provenance for AI training teams

#VoiceAI #DataSecurity #AICompliance #SyntheticMedia #B2BSaaS

The Problem

AI contractors, voice actors, and annotation vendors risk having raw voice datasets copied, leaked, or reused without consent, making it hard to prove ownership, limit usage, and respond to breaches.

Our Solution

VoiceVault gives teams and talent a secure vault for voice collection with consent records, usage policies, watermarking, access logs, and breach response workflows so every clip has provable provenance and permitted use.

Key Features

  • Per-speaker consent and licensing records
  • Watermarked audio exports and provenance IDs
  • Dataset access logs with anomaly alerts
  • Usage policy templates for model training rights
  • Instant takedown and breach response audit packs
